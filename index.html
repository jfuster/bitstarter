Based on Google Glass, Sport Sight will add some features specially designed for runners, ciclysts, mountain lovers, and definitely all the people who love sports.

Going for a run? Track your route, enjoy your music, load your training and go for it!

Riding your bicycle right now? Receive real time traffic alerts and plan your route with the GPS.

Enjoying the mountain? Avoid the surprises, receive the weather forecast for the next hours and let your beloved ones know where are you with the web app GPS tracking.

Use the Polar WearLink© band with Bluetooth to monitorize properly your heart rate.

Define your training heart rate limits and set warnings in the upper limit.

Compare in real time your heart rate with other similar sessions to check your progression.

Use your Sport Sights whether it's a sunny or a cloudy day, just add a proper lents.

Your Sport Sights will determine which sport are you doing and will adjust it's configuration to show you the most valuable information. On the other hand, will disable some functions like the music while you are on your bike.

FAQ

What's Sport Sight?
It's just an app for Google Glass, which enhances some cool features for the people who do sport.
What if I don't have Google Glass?
Don't worry, you can buy them with the latest Sport Sight app preloaded directly from our store.
Is Sport Sight suitable for all sports?
Neither Google Glass nor Sport Sight works under water yet... Currenly we support running, cycling, hiking and walking. Curling as well as other sports will be added soon :)
